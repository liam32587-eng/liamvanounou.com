Who This Is For

Built for serious people.

This isn't a mass program. I work with a small number of people who are genuinely committed to moving better.

This is for you if...

✓

You're 25–40 and want to learn to move like a gymnast — handstands, rings, real body control.

✓

You're serious about training and show up consistently — not someone looking for shortcuts.

✓

You've been stuck on the same plateau and need structure, accountability, and expert eyes on your form.

✓

You want to build real gymnastic strength — ring dips, L-sits, planche progressions, freestanding handstands.

✓

You're willing to invest in yourself and your training — and understand quality takes time.

This is NOT for you if...

✗

You want quick results with no consistency or effort on your end.

✗

You're looking for a generic cookie-cutter plan you could find for free on YouTube.

✗

You're not ready to film yourself training and engage with the feedback process.

✗

You have an injury that prevents you from training — see a physiotherapist first.
